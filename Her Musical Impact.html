HBCU

Beyonce's involvment within the HBCU has been nothing but impactful. She had always included the elements of the matching bands, which were way to give back and aknowledge the musical culture of using live instruments which is a huge part of Black American.

She was the first black lady in history to headline the concert in 19 years! It was the viewed Coachella performance in history. She utilized an all black team and put on a show that left a long lasting impact in performance history. She will forever be celebrated in HBCU spaces.
